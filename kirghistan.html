A nomad culture

The World nomad games, games inspired by nomadic culture, have allowed traditions to continue. They take place in the Kirchin valley and on the shores of Lake Isssy kul. They take place every 4 years since 2014. Kok-Boru, horse racing, Falconry, yurt assembly, archery, are among the many games in competition.

Mes Pérégrinations

Norway.

Located in the north of Norway beyond the polar circle, Lofoten are made up of five islands (Moskenesøy, Flakstadøy, Vestvagøy, Gimsøy and Austvagøy) linked together by bridges or tunnels. This archipelago, which is reached after several hours by ferry, lives to the rhythm of cod fishing, as evidenced by the old fishing huts on stilts (rorbu) and the many fish dryers that you come across everywhere in the islands. There are incredible landscapes, between sea and mountains with sharp peaks, you can hike there and admire fjords with deep blue colors, sandy beaches with turquoise waters. I was able to admire magnificent Northern Lights as magical as they are mysterious.

Northern Lights

What admiration in front of these Northern Lights as magical as they are mysterious. It is easy to imagine that these phenomena could have been associated with myths, beliefs or legends. What a fascination to see these lights evolve, transform over the minutes or hours.
